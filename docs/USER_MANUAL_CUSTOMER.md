# Vybe Customer App User Manual

## Getting Started

### 1. Download & Install
- **Android**: Download from Google Play Store - search "Vybe"
- **iOS**: Download from Apple App Store - search "Vybe"

### 2. Account Registration

#### Login via OTP
1. Open the Vybe app
2. Enter your mobile number (with country code: +91 for India)
3. Tap **"Send OTP"**
4. Enter the 6-digit OTP received via SMS
5. Enter your full name (first time only)
6. Tap **"Verify & Login"**

---

## Booking a Ride

### Step 1: Set Pickup Location
You can set your pickup location using any of these methods:

#### Option A: Current GPS Location
1. On the home screen, tap **"Use Current Location"**
2. The map will center on your current position
3. Confirm the pickup address shown

#### Option B: Search Address
1. Tap the **"Where to pick up?"** search bar
2. Type your pickup address or landmark
3. Select from the autocomplete suggestions
4. The pin drops on the selected location

#### Option C: Drop a Pin
1. Long-press anywhere on the map
2. A pin drops at that location
3. The address is automatically fetched
4. Drag the pin to adjust if needed

### Step 2: Set Drop Location

#### Option A: Search Destination
1. Tap **"Where to go?"** search bar
2. Type your destination
3. Select from autocomplete suggestions

#### Option B: Drop a Pin
1. Switch to **"Drop Pin"** mode
2. Long-press on the map at your destination
3. Confirm the address

### Step 3: View Fare Estimate
- The app shows:
  - **Total distance** (km)
  - **Estimated time** (mins)
  - **Base fare** breakdown
  - **Total fare** (₹)
  - **Promo code** option (if available)

### Step 4: Apply Promo Code (Optional)
1. Tap **"Apply Promo"**
2. Enter your promo code
3. Tap **"Apply"**
4. Discount is reflected in the fare

### Step 5: Book Your Ride
1. Review fare and route
2. Select payment method: **Cash** or **UPI**
3. Tap **"Book Vybe"**
4. Wait while we find a nearby driver

## During the Ride

### Live Tracking
- Watch your driver's location in real-time on the map
- Estimated time of arrival (ETA) updates live
- Driver's name, photo, and vehicle details shown

### Contact Driver
- **Call**: Tap the phone icon to call your driver
- **Chat**: Tap the chat icon to send text messages
- Share your location or ask questions

### Share Trip
1. Tap **"Share Trip"**
2. Choose a contact or app
3. A live tracking link is shared
4. Your contact can track your ride in real-time

### SOS Emergency
1. Tap the **SOS** button (red icon)
2. Confirm emergency
3. Alert is sent to:
   - Vybe security team
   - Your emergency contacts
   - (Optional: local authorities)
4. Live location is shared with responders

---

## After the Ride

### Payment

#### Cash
1. Pay the driver directly in cash
2. Amount shown on the app matches the fare
3. Tap **"Confirm Payment"** to complete

#### UPI (Razorpay)
1. A payment request appears
2. Open your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
3. Complete the payment
4. Payment confirmed automatically

### Rate Your Driver
1. Rate from 1-5 stars
2. Optionally leave a review comment
3. Tap **"Submit"**

## Ride History

1. Tap the menu icon (☰)
2. Select **"My Rides"**
3. View all past rides with:
   - Date & time
   - Pickup & drop locations
   - Fare paid
   - Driver details
   - Your rating

---

## Saved Places

### Add Home/Work
1. Go to **"Saved Places"** from menu
2. Tap **"Add Home"** or **"Add Work"**
3. Search or pin the location
4. Save for quick booking

### Favorite Locations
- Add frequently visited places
- Quick-select when booking
- Edit or delete anytime

---

## Referral Program

### Invite Friends
1. Go to **"Refer & Earn"** from menu
2. Share your referral code: `ZR-XXXXXXX`
3. Friend enters code during registration
4. You earn ₹50 when they complete 3 rides
5. Your friend gets a discount on their first ride

---

## Promo Codes

1. Go to **"Promotions"** from menu
2. View available offers
3. Apply promo code before booking
4. Discount automatically applied

## Notifications

- **Ride Requested**: Confirmation of booking
- **Driver Assigned**: Driver details sent
- **Driver Arrived**: Driver reached pickup
- **Ride Started**: Journey begins
- **Ride Completed**: Trip ended
- **Payment**: Receipt of payment
- **Promotions**: Special offers & discounts

## Support

1. Go to **"Help & Support"** from menu
2. Browse FAQs
3. Tap **"Contact Support"**
4. Select issue category
5. Describe your issue
6. Support team responds within 1 hour

- **Notifications**: Toggle push notifications

- **About**: App version, terms, licenses

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| OTP not received | Wait 30s, tap Resend. Check network signal |
| Map not loading | Check internet, enable GPS |
| Can't book ride | Ensure pickup and drop are set |
| Payment failed | Check UPI balance, try again |
| Driver not moving | Contact driver via chat/call |
| App crashing | Update to latest version, restart |
