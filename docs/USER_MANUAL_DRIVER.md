# Vybe Driver App User Manual

## Getting Started

### Download & Install
- **Android**: Download from Google Play Store - search "Vybe Driver"
- A smartphone with GPS, camera, and internet is required

---

## Registration Process

### Step 1: Create Account
1. Open Vybe Driver app
2. Enter your mobile number (+91 for India)
3. Tap **"Send OTP"**
4. Enter the 6-digit OTP
5. Enter your full name
6. Tap **"Register"**

### Step 2: Upload Documents
Upload clear photos of the following:

#### Aadhaar Card
1. Tap **"Upload Aadhaar"**
2. Take a photo of the **front side**
3. Take a photo of the **back side**
4. Ensure all details are clearly visible

#### E-Rickshaw RC (Registration Certificate)
1. Tap **"Upload RC"**
2. Take photos of RC front and back
3. Ensure registration number is visible
4. Verify vehicle details match your E-Rickshaw

#### Live Selfie
1. Tap **"Take Selfie"**
2. Position your face within the frame
3. Ensure good lighting
4. Tap to capture

### Step 3: Complete Profile
1. Enter your date of birth
2. Select gender
3. Enter your full address
4. City, State, Pincode
5. Tap **"Save Profile"**

### Step 4: Register Vehicle
1. Enter E-Rickshaw registration number
2. Select vehicle make and model
3. Enter manufacturing year
4. Enter battery capacity (e.g., "100 Ah")
5. Enter range per charge (km)
6. Enter seating capacity
7. Upload vehicle photo
8. Tap **"Save Vehicle"**

### Step 5: Pay Registration Fee

#### Current Fee
- **Promotional Fee**: ₹499 (limited-time offer)
- **Standard Fee**: ₹999 (after promotion ends)

#### Payment via UPI
1. Tap **"Pay Registration Fee"**
2. UPI payment screen opens
3. Pay using any UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
4. Payment is processed instantly

### Step 6: Submit Application
1. Review all documents and information
2. Tap **"Submit for Review"**
3. Application status: **"Pending Review"**
4. You will be notified when reviewed

### Step 7: Wait for Approval
- **Processing time**: 24-48 hours (typically)
- You'll receive a push notification when:
  - ✅ **Approved**: You can start accepting rides
  - ❌ **Rejected**: Reason provided, you can reapply

## Going Online

### Prerequisites
- ✅ Registration approved
- ✅ Documents verified
- ✅ GPS enabled
- ✅ Internet connection

### Go Online
1. On the home screen, tap **"Go Online"**
2. Toggle turns green ✅
3. You are now visible to nearby customers
4. Ride requests will appear automatically

### Go Offline
1. Tap **"Go Offline"** when done
2. Toggle turns gray
3. No more ride requests
4. You can still view earnings

---

## Receiving & Managing Rides

### New Ride Request
When a ride request arrives:
1. **Loud notification** sounds
2. **60 seconds** to accept or reject
3. Shows:
   - Pickup location
   - Drop location
   - Distance
   - Estimated fare
   - Customer rating (if available)

### Accept Ride
1. Tap **"Accept"** (green button)
2. Customer is notified
3. Navigation starts automatically

### Reject Ride
1. Tap **"Reject"** (red button)
2. Ride goes to next available driver
3. No penalty for rejecting

---

## Ride Execution

### Navigate to Pickup
1. Google Maps opens automatically
2. Follow navigation to pickup location
3. Customer sees your ETA in real-time

### Arrive at Pickup
1. Tap **"Arrived at Pickup"**
2. Customer is notified
3. Wait up to 5 minutes (free waiting)
4. After 5 min, waiting charges apply (₹2/min)

### Start Ride
1. Ensure customer is seated
2. Tap **"Start Ride"**
3. Ride officially begins
4. Customer can see the route and your location

### End Ride
1. Arrive at drop location
2. Tap **"End Ride"**
3. Ride is marked complete
4. Fare is calculated and confirmed
5. Ask customer for payment (cash) or wait for UPI

---

## Payment Handling

### Cash Payments
1. Accept cash from customer
2. Amount matches app-displayed fare
3. Customer confirms payment in app
4. Amount credited to your earnings

### UPI Payments
1. Customer pays via UPI
2. Payment is processed automatically
3. You receive instant notification
4. No need to handle cash

### Your Earnings
- **Total Fare** = Customer pays this
- **Commission** = 10% (Vybe platform fee)
- **Your Earnings** = Total Fare - Commission

*Example: Ride fare ₹100*
- Commission: ₹10 (10%)
- You earn: ₹90

## Ratings

### Your Rating
- Average of all customer ratings
- Displayed on your profile
- 5.0 is perfect, aim for 4.5+

### Tips for 5-Star Ratings
- Be on time
- Drive safely and smoothly
- Be polite and helpful
- Keep your E-Rickshaw clean
- Use GPS navigation
- Confirm drop location before ending ride

## Support

### Need Help?
1. Tap **"Support"** from menu
2. Browse common topics
3. Tap **"Contact Us"**
4. Describe your issue
5. Support team responds within 1 hour

### Common Issues
| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Can't go online | Ensure GPS & internet on. Account must be approved |
| No ride requests | Try moving to a busier area |
| Payment not received | Check with customer, contact support |
| App is slow | Clear cache, restart app |
| Document rejected | Re-upload clear photos |

## Pro Tips

1. **Peak Hours**: 8-10 AM & 5-8 PM for maximum rides
2. **High-demand Areas**: Railway stations, bus stands, markets, colleges
3. **Battery Management**: Charge fully before starting your shift
4. **Customer Service**: A smile goes a long way
5. **App Updates**: Keep app updated for latest features
6. **Data Saving**: Use WiFi when available for updates
